Reading Speed

RSVP Speed Reading

Read one word at a time at increasing speeds. Eliminate subvocalization and train faster comprehension through Rapid Serial Visual Presentation.

Reading SpeedAttentional FocusProcessing Fluency

Function Trained

Reading speed, attentional focus, and processing fluency by eliminating eye saccades and reducing subvocalization.

Real-Life Benefits

  • Significantly faster reading speeds
  • Reduced subvocalization (inner reading voice)
  • Improved sustained focus under time pressure
  • Better comprehension at higher reading speeds

Instructions

  1. Words from a passage appear one at a time in the center of the screen.
  2. Keep your eyes fixed — do not scan or move your gaze.
  3. Adjust WPM speed with the slider to find your optimal edge.
  4. Start slow and increase gradually as comprehension remains intact.
  5. The goal is fluency and comprehension, not just raw speed.
