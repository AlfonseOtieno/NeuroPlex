Visual Processing

Schulte Tables

Find numbers in order as fast as possible across a randomized grid. Train your visual scanning speed and peripheral awareness.

Visual Scanning Processing Speed Focus Peripheral Awareness

Function Trained

Visual scanning, peripheral field awareness, and processing speed through rapid sequential search across a visual grid.

Real-Life Benefits

  • Faster visual field scanning for reading and driving
  • Improved peripheral awareness in daily life
  • Enhanced attentional switching
  • Used by elite athletes, pilots, and military personnel

Instructions

  1. Click numbers in order from 1 to the grid maximum — do not skip.
  2. Find each number as quickly as you can without moving your head.
  3. Use peripheral vision — avoid scanning every cell individually.
  4. Work from the center of the grid outward to engage your full peripheral field.
  5. Aim to beat your completion time on each attempt.
